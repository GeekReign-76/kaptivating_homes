Overview

All seven campaigns share the same landing page: /relocate. The page features community-specific guides for Indian/South Asian, Hispanic/Latino, Vietnamese, Korean, African/Caribbean, and Middle Eastern families, plus a luxury lifestyle section and a lead capture popup. Each campaign below targets a different audience or intent signal, with distinct ad copy, keywords, and bidding strategy.

Recommended starting budget: $20–$40/day per campaign. Run 2–3 simultaneously and pause the lowest performers after 30 days.

Shared Settings Across All Campaigns

  • Bidding strategy: Start with Manual CPC or Enhanced CPC; switch to Target CPA once you have 30+ conversions
  • Conversion tracking: Set up on the contact form submission, chat widget session start, and appointment booking confirmation
  • Ad schedule: Run 7am–10pm Mon–Sat; 9am–8pm Sunday (adjust based on data)
  • Device: All devices enabled; mobile bid adjustment +10% (relocation searches happen heavily on mobile during commute/lunch)
  • Negative keywords (add to all campaigns): rental, apartment, rent, jobs, careers, hotel, airbnb, vacation, cheap, studio, roommate

Landing Page Notes for Conversion

The /relocate page already has the PropertyInterestPrompt popup — make sure Google Ads conversion tracking fires on:

  1. Lead form submission (Step 1 of the popup — share a property)
  2. Appointment booking confirmation (Step 2 — book)
  3. Chat widget session initiated

Without conversion tracking, Google's Smart Bidding cannot optimize spend toward actual leads.
